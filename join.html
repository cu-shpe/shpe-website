Why Join SHPE?

SHPE Benefits

Internships:
The National SHPE Conference is the largest Hispanic Technical Conference in the Nation, and takes place once a year, attracting hundreds of employers from all different industries who are looking to hire for internships, co-ops, full-time positions, and also recruit for graduate programs. Coupled with the online database of openings on oneSHPE.org, SHPE provides members with a variety of opportunities to gain work experience

Build/Expand your Network:
SHPE offers members several events throughout the year, which provide opportunities to meet and connect with other SHPE members across the country, from other students (undergrad and grad) and professional members, to corporate leaders and SHPE leaders; including mentoring from these leaders.

Scholarships:
SHPE members qualify to apply for Undergraduate and Graduate scholarship opportunities ranging from $1,000 to $5,000 (pending eligibility criteria), in addition to applying for scholarships to attend the SHPE National Conference and the National Leadership (NILA) Conference.

Leadership Opportunities:
As a member of SHPE, you can become a leader at the university (LSU) level, the Regional level, and the National level. Joining the SHPE network can be a great asset throughout your academic and professional careers.

Professional/Career Development:
Competition opportunities to test and refine your academic and technical talent. GRE/GRAD Lab to guide you through the graduate school application process. Discounted registration to SHPE National, Regional & Local events. Complimentary subscriptions to SHPE Magazine and Hispanic Business Magazine. Complimentary SHPE stoles upon graduation.
